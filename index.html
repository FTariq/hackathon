What is WarwickHACK?

OPEN TO ALL, NO CODING KNOWLEDGE NEEDED!

Since 2014, WarwickTECH has been organising the University’s flagship hackathon and 2019 will be no exception!

Coding, learning, having a blast: WarwickHACK embraces them all… and even more! Expect an unforgettable experience, complemented with free food/meals and cool prizes from our sponsors!

Whether you're a seasoned hackathon veteran or have never been to a hackathon before, WarwickHACK is the best way to meet other tech enthusiasts and get started on a cool project! You will be assisted by experienced coders, industry mentors and friendly organisers. No coding background is needed!

How do I take part?

To register for WarwickHACK and attend the event, you just need to grab a free ticket from Eventbrite (the link is above). Tickets are regularly being released, so like the Facebook page to know when the next release batch is happening!

This is a proper hackathon, so please bring your laptop and any other bits and bobs you may need to work with.

You must be enrolled on a University course to attend.

If you have questions, accessibility concerns or require any adjustments, please contact events@warwick.tech.
